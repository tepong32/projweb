Hi there! I am Teppy and this dog with me is Tico!
In the light of the COVID-19 global pandemic outbreak in 2020 (specifically in March here in the Philippines), I decided to try to create a website. (Yeah... I just tried!) This was because I already had my eye on learning programming using Python a year or two prior and I already am quite knowledgeable of its basics (I was trying to create my own Android app a few months before the pandemic broke out, too. Seems like I failed at it, tho.)
Hey! Did I mention I just searched for "learning programming" on YouTube (2017, I think...) while working as a "call-center agent"? Also, I am a Business Ad graduate (bragging rights?).
Cutting-off all the umimportant parts of the story: I just decided that since there will be quite a boring time spending days at home doing barely "just anything", I'll try to be as productive as I can be. I also came across this quote saying:

"If you don't get a skill or improve one during this home quarantine period, you never lacked time; YOU LACKED DISCIPLINE."

...and the rest is what can be seen on this website. ;)
